FONT NOTES

Monkey Island font - Best used at 8pt
   from https://www.ptless.org/sfomi/
   © 1990 Lucasfilm Games

Munro - Best used at 10pt

Crawl fonts - Best used at 16pt

The fonts in the demo are all intended for pixel art games-
For high res games, set "Font Size" higher in the Text Mesh, and change "Character Size" to 1 (or down until it's crisp enough)
